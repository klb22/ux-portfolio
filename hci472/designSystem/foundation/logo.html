Logo Variations

Color Logo

The color logo is the primary logo that will be used across all brand assets.

Inversed Logo

The inversed logo is an inversed version of the color logo that can be used against light backgrounds.

Black and White Logo

The black and white logo can be used in light mode.

Inversed B&W Logo

The inversed black and white logo can be used in dark mode.

Favicon

My favicon is a small version of my logo used in browser tabs.
